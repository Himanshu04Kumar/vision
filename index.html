01 — The Problem

India's post-warranty repair experience is fundamentally broken.

Every device owner in India faces the same impossible choice when their electronics break after warranty expires. There is no trustworthy, fairly priced, transparent middle ground — and nobody has built it at scale.

700+ million smartphone users. Hundreds of millions of laptops. All aging. All eventually needing repair. The organised, trustworthy segment of this market is less than 2% of the total.
Option A

Local Shops

Cheap but unvetted. Opaque pricing. Counterfeit parts with no way to verify. Zero accountability when something goes wrong.

The Gap

No trustworthy middle ground exists.

This is the entire opportunity. A market of hundreds of millions of devices with no credible, transparent repair option.

Option B

Authorised Centres

Trustworthy but 2–3x overpriced. Long wait times. Poor communication. Inaccessible for most device owners.

07 — Long-term Vision

Not a repair shop. The operating system for device aftercare in India.

Where this goes
Every electronic device sold in India. Serviced, recycled, and powered by Cepheus.
The repair network is the entry point. The platform — carrying device data, repair history, warranty status, parts intelligence — is the actual business. When we know what every device in India looks like, when it fails, and what it costs to fix, we become indispensable to every brand that sells into this market.

As the repair network scales, it generates two things most companies never get: a pan-India physical presence and a mountain of e-waste. Both become businesses in their own right. The e-waste from millions of repaired devices feeds a structured recycling and refurbishment operation — turning what was cost into revenue, and what was environmental liability into an ESG asset.

The long horizon — and we mean long — is vertical integration into the semiconductor supply chain. India is building its semiconductor ambitions. A company that understands device failure at the component level, that has repaired millions of boards and chips, that has catalogued parts failures across every major brand in the market, is uniquely positioned to contribute to and benefit from that national infrastructure. This is not a near-term plan. It is the destination that makes every earlier decision make sense.
